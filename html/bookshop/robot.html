I, Robot (Isaac Asimov)

Overview Contents First Edition

I, Robot is a collection of nine science fiction short stories by Isaac Asimov. The stories originally appeared in the American magazines Super Science Stories and Astounding Science Fiction between 1940 and 1950 and were then compiled into a book for stand-alone publication by Gnome Press in 1950, in an initial edition of 5,000 copies.

  1. Robbie
  2. Runaround
  3. Reason
  4. Catch That Rabbit
  5. Liar!
  6. Little Lost Robot
  7. Escape!
  8. Evidence
  9. The Evitable Conflict
